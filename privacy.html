Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stamped ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. Stamped is a travel tracker that lets you record the countries and regions you visit.

The short version: Your travel data (visits, notes, photos, and location) stays on your device — we have no user accounts and no servers that collect it. The app does, however, show ads through Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers (such as your advertising ID) to serve ads. See "Advertising" below.

1. Data stored on your device

Everything you create in Stamped is stored locally on your device only:

  • Country, region, and Japanese prefecture visits (codes and visit dates)
  • Text notes you write for a country
  • Photos you attach to notes
  • App settings (language, theme, wish list, achievements, streaks)

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it (see "Backups" below). We have no access to it.

2. Location information

With your permission, Stamped reads your device location when you open the app to detect which country, region, or Japanese prefecture you are currently in. Stamped does not track your location in the background or while the app is closed. This processing happens entirely on your device: your location is converted into a country/region code (for example, jp or JP-13) and a visit date, which are saved locally. Your GPS coordinates are never transmitted off the device. You can also use the app without granting location access by marking visits manually.

3. Photos

Photos you attach to notes are stored on your device only. We do not access your photo library beyond the images you explicitly select, and photos are never uploaded to us.

4. No accounts; we don't collect your travel data

  • There are no user accounts and no sign-in.
  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your visits, notes, photos, or location on any server of ours.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • The app shows ads via Google AdMob, which collects limited device data for advertising (see "Advertising").

5. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Stamped displays banner ads provided by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, the AdMob SDK may collect and process certain information from your device, such as your advertising identifier, IP address, device type and coarse, non-precise usage data. This data is handled by Google under its own policies, and may be used for personalized or non-personalized advertising.

  • How Google uses data: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking).

We do not share your in-app travel data (visits, notes, photos) with AdMob or any other party — that data stays on your device.

6. Backups (you control your data)

Stamped lets you export your data to a backup file (a .zip) and import it again — for example, to move it to a new device. These backup files are created and stored wherever you choose on your device or in your own storage. We never receive or store your backups.

7. Map data

To display maps, the app may download map imagery from third-party map tile providers. Such requests are handled by those providers under their own privacy policies and do not include your personal travel data.

8. Your choices

  • You can revoke location and photo permissions at any time in your device settings.
  • You can delete individual visits, notes, and photos within the app.
  • You can clear all data from within the app, or by uninstalling it.

9. Data retention

Because all data is stored only on your device, it remains until you delete it in the app or uninstall the app. Uninstalling the app removes its local data.

10. Children's privacy

Stamped is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their personal data.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

12. Contact us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at devbyryosuke2025@gmail.com.
