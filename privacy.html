Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stamped ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. Stamped is a travel tracker that lets you record the countries and regions you visit. We designed it to keep as much of your data on your device as possible.

The short version: Your precise GPS location is used only on your device to figure out which country or region you're in. We never send raw coordinates to our servers. If you sign in, only your derived visit records (e.g. "visited Japan") are synced so you can back them up and restore them.

1. Information we collect

a. Location information

With your permission, Stamped reads your device location (including in the background, if you allow it) to automatically detect which country, region, or Japanese prefecture you are in. This processing happens on your device. We convert your location into a country/region code (for example, jp or JP-13) and a visit date. Raw GPS coordinates are not transmitted to or stored on our servers. You can use the app without granting location access by marking visits manually.

b. Account information (only if you sign in)

Signing in is optional and is only needed for cloud backup and sync. If you sign in with Google or LINE, we receive and store basic profile information from that provider:

  • A provider account identifier
  • Your email address
  • Your display name
  • Your profile picture URL (avatar)

c. Travel data

If you sign in, the following data you create in the app is synced to our servers:

DataExample / contents
Country visitsCountry code, visit dates, total time present, declarations
Region & prefecture visitsRegion/prefecture codes and visit dates
NotesText notes you write for a country
PhotosPhotos you choose to attach, included in your backup archive
BackupsA single ZIP backup file per account and its metadata (filename, size, upload time)

If you do not sign in, all of this data stays only on your device and is not sent to us.

d. Photos

Photos you attach to notes are stored on your device. They are only uploaded to our servers if you create a cloud backup. We do not access your photo library beyond the images you explicitly select.

e. Notifications

With your permission, the app sends local notifications (for example, when you arrive in a new country or reach a milestone). These are generated on your device.

2. How we use information

  • To detect and record the places you visit.
  • To display your map, statistics, achievements, timeline, and streaks.
  • To back up your data and sync it across your devices when you sign in.
  • To operate, maintain, and secure the service.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for advertising or third-party tracking.

3. Sharing and third parties

We share data only in these limited cases:

  • Sign-in providers (Google, LINE): used to authenticate you. Their handling of your data is governed by their own privacy policies.
  • Hosting infrastructure: our backend and database run on servers that store your synced data on our behalf.
  • Legal requirements: when required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

4. Data retention

On-device data remains until you delete it or uninstall the app. If you sign in, your synced data is kept until you delete it or request account deletion. When you delete your account, your associated records (visits, notes, backups) are removed from our servers.

5. Your rights and choices

  • You can revoke location, notification, and photo permissions at any time in your device settings.
  • You can delete individual visits, notes, and photos within the app.
  • You can request export or deletion of your account data by contacting us.
  • You can use the app without an account, keeping all data local.

6. Security

Data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Children's privacy

Stamped is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their personal data.

8. International users

Your data may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country. By using the app, you consent to this transfer and processing.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact us
