Stamp your journey
across the world.

Stamped turns every country and region you visit into a collectible stamp on your personal world map. Travel, and watch the map fill in.

What you can do

🗺️

Collect the world

Open the app when you arrive and your country is colored in automatically, or mark them by hand. See your coverage grow over time.

🇯🇵

Down to the prefecture

Go beyond countries with detailed Japanese prefecture tracking and regional maps.

🏅

Achievements & streaks

Earn badges, build travel streaks, and hit continent milestones as you explore.

📸

Notes & photo wall

Attach memories and photos to each country, and revisit them on a timeline and calendar.

📊

Your travel stats

See countries visited, time spent, and a breakdown of your trips at a glance.

🔒

Privacy-first

Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking — your travels are yours alone.

Ready to start collecting?

No sign-up, no account — just open the app and start filling in your world map. Your data stays on your device, and you can export a backup anytime.
