Frequently asked questions

How does Stamped know which countries I've visited?

When you open the app, Stamped reads your current device location and matches it to a country or region on your device. The matching happens locally — only the resulting country/region is recorded. You can also add or remove visits manually at any time.

Does Stamped track me in the background?

No. Stamped only checks your location while you have the app open. It does not track your location in the background or while the app is closed, so remember to open the app when you arrive somewhere new.

Do I have to share my location?

No. Location access is optional. Without it, automatic detection is turned off, but you can still mark every country and region by hand.

Do I need an account?

No. Stamped has no accounts and no sign-in. It works fully offline, and all your data is stored on your device only.

How do I back up or move my data to a new device?

Open Settings and use the backup export to save a ZIP file of your data, which you can keep anywhere you like. On a new device, use the import option to restore from that file. Your backups stay with you — they are never uploaded to us.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual visits, notes, and photos in the app, clear all data from Settings, or simply uninstall the app. Since everything is stored on your device, that removes it completely — there is nothing stored on our side to delete.

My location seems wrong or a country didn't register.

Location estimates can occasionally be off near borders or with weak GPS. You can correct any entry manually. Make sure location permission is granted and that you open the app while you're at the new location.

What languages does Stamped support?

Stamped is available in many languages and follows your device language automatically.
