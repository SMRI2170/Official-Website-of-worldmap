Frequently asked questions

How does Stamped know which countries I've visited?

With your permission, Stamped reads your device location and matches it to a country or region on your device. The matching happens locally — only the resulting country/region is recorded. You can also add or remove visits manually at any time.

Do I have to share my location?

No. Location access is optional. Without it, automatic detection is turned off, but you can still mark every country and region by hand.

Why does the app ask for background location?

Background location lets Stamped record a new country even when the app isn't open — useful while you're traveling. You can decline it and use the app with "while using" permission or fully manual entry.

Do I need an account?

No. The app works fully offline with no sign-in. An account (Google or LINE) is only needed if you want to back up your map to the cloud and sync it across devices.

How do I back up or restore my data?

Open Settings to create a backup. If you're signed in, your data and a ZIP backup are stored to your account so you can restore them on a new device. You can also export a backup file locally.

How do I delete my account and data?

You can delete individual visits, notes, and photos in the app. To delete your entire account and the data synced to our servers, email devbyryosuke2025@gmail.com from your registered email address and we'll process the request.

My location seems wrong or a country didn't register.

Location estimates can occasionally be off near borders or with weak GPS. You can correct any entry manually. Make sure location permission is granted and that battery saver isn't restricting the app in the background.

What languages does Stamped support?

Stamped is available in many languages and follows your device language automatically.
